Hockey Stick Features:

Hockey sticks come in many sizes and styles for any type of hockey player. Hockey stick sizes range from youth to senior, and they can be made from composite materials like high-density carbon fiber or wood. Composite hockey sticks are the preferred choice for athletes looking for the lightest stick possible, while wood ice hockey sticks are best for those wanting economical equipment for the casual player. There are a few parts to an ice hockey stick you should be aware of. A hockey stick blade can have different curves that can be tailored to your game, and hockey stick shafts have different flex ratings, which reference the amount of pressure necessary to flex the stick.
